F A S H I O N F I N I T Y

A Fashion blog by Trina Mukherjee


This blog aims to express my current thoughts on trends, garments, street style, or anything about Indian Ethnic Wears. I'd also like to offer some style advice by sharing some interesting facts about fashion and styles in this blog.

This blog also focuses on the fact that Fashion is not gender specific. Women or men both have their own sense of style and fashion. Attitude is everything. Attention to detail is of utmost importance when you want to look good.

You could find yourself unable to explain something as mysterious as fashion. As we know, Design is an evolving process that involves a series of creative ideas.




ABOUT ME


Hi ! I am a Bengali girl, lives in Kolkata, India and has a passion for fashion and style. In my free time, I like travelling or reading fashion magazines. I always admire individuality. To me, style is considerably more fascinating than fashion.

Style is always a choice, so make yours!!
